6. Add onions, garlic, and red peppers to the skillet and sauté until tender, about 5-7 minutes.

7. Whisk together chicken broth and flour in a small bowl. Add the mixture to the skillet and bring to a boil.

8. Reduce heat and simmer for 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.

9. Return chicken breasts to the skillet and top with the red pepper mixture.

10. Cut butter into small pieces and dot on top of the chicken and vegetables.

11. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through.

12. Garnish with parsley and serve hot. Enjoy!
